Hila Chefer

Hi there! I am a Ph.D. Candidate at Tel Aviv University, working in the Deep Learning Lab under the supervision of Prof. Lior Wolf. Also, I am currently a research intern at Google Research in Tel Aviv. My research is centered around computer vision and multi-modal learning. I am particularly passionate about developing methods to understand deep neural networks and leveraging their insights to enhance the expressiveness, robustness, and fairness of the model.
